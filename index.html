Trivia Quiz App

Test your knowledge across multiple categories with questions powered by the Open Trivia Database API.

How It Works

Choose a trivia category, answer a set of questions, and see your score at the end. Your recent scores are automatically saved in your browser and can be viewed anytime on the “Result” page.

  • Select a category to begin your quiz.
  • Answer multiple-choice questions from that category.
  • Review your past results to track your progress.

About the Open Trivia Database

The Open Trivia Database provides an open, community-driven collection of trivia questions. You can even contribute your own questions to help expand the database for everyone.

Each category includes a variety of questions of different difficulties and topics, ensuring a fresh and challenging experience every time.

Technologies Used

  • Bootstrap 5 — for responsive layout and modern UI styling. Learn more
  • Open Trivia DB API — for fetching real trivia questions and categories. API documentation
  • JavaScript — for interactive logic and score tracking using session storage.

Categories Available

The app offers multiple trivia categories including General Knowledge, Science, History, Sports, and more. The exact list of categories and question counts are dynamically fetched from the Open Trivia Database.
